## What We Need From You to Begin

A few things so we can start.

We can start as soon as your first payment lands. Here is the checklist of what we need. Send what you have now, and we will help you with the rest.

This is one checklist in two parts. Here is the full list.

### Part 1

**Your brand**

- **Logo files.** The biggest, clearest version you have.
- Your brand colors and any fonts you use.
- Photos: your clinic, your products, your team, and a headshot of you.
- How you want the helper to talk. For example, warm and friendly, or calm and proper.
- Anything that shows your style, like an old website, a flyer, or an ad.

**Your two businesses (Wellspring Clinic and SightSage)**

- Name, address, phone, email, and open hours for each one.
- **For Wellspring, send us:**
  - A list of what you do for patients.
  - Your staff, with one or two lines about each person.
  - How people book a visit.
  - The questions you want new patients to answer first.
  - What happens if someone is late or skips a visit.

**Your logins**

- We will send you a safe way to share these. Never put a password in a normal email.
- Your website and hosting, for both sites.
- Your Shopify store (for SightSage).
- Your Jane App login, so the helper can book visits into your calendar.
- Your web address login (sometimes called your domain).
- Your Google Business page.
- An email the helper can send from.

### Part 2

**Your products (SightSage). This part takes the most time. For each product, tell us:**

- What it is and what it does.
- How it helps the customer.
- Who it is for.
- The price, the sizes, and how to use it.
- The questions customers ask the most.
- What problems it fixes. Why some people are not sure about buying it.
- A clear photo.
- Stock, shipping, and returns.
- A sheet or file is perfect. Just send what you have.

**Your clinic care (Wellspring). For each treatment, tell us:**

- What it helps with, and what happens at a visit.
- Who it is for.
- The questions patients ask the most, and the problems it solves.
- Care notes for before and after.
- The price, how long it takes, and if people can book online.

**Helper rules and your point person**

- The languages you want. English, and Chinese if you like.
- The deal for a first order. Tell us how much money people save, and when they can use it.
- Tell us any words it must always use. Tell us any words it must never use. It will never tell people what is wrong with their health.
- One person we can reach, and the best way to reach them: text, email, or call.

**You do not need all of this on day one.** Send what you have, and we will help you find the rest.
