## Collections Overview

Across all of them the site reinforces a consistent house frame: "premium products designed for your wellness," free shipping to the USA & Canada on orders over $99, worldwide shipping, and a recurring push toward "Subscribe & Save" and the loyalty program ("Vision Club"). ### Cross-collection observations
- **Sparse category copy:** Most collections have no dedicated descriptive paragraph; positioning is carried by product grouping, collection name, ratings, and the global house messaging.
- **Heavy product overlap / laddering:** Single products roll up into bundles (e.g., dry eye relief mild→moderate→severe; hair products into Revive and Thrive / Slim Revive Thrive), and items appear across multiple collections (Bestsellers, Eye Health, Vision Cookies, Bundles all share SKUs).
- **Consistent commercial frame:** Free shipping over $99 (USA/Canada), worldwide shipping, Subscribe & Save, and Vision Club loyalty appear sitewide.
- **Price/format tiers:** Entry gummies/cookies and content products at the low end (~$36–$52), core supplements mid (~$71–$130), and premium bundles/services/herbal supply at the top (up to $568 supply and $494 bundle).
- **Eye health is the hub:** Vision-related products (SightC, Blueberry Gummies, Veggie Cookies, dry eye bundles) anchor the catalog, consistent with the brand name; hair and weight/wellness are adjacent expansion categories.
