## Summary

The Wellness Bundle is a SightSage combo of two "made in Canada" supplements — the Superfood Wellness Blend (a powder of ~29-31 whole fruits, vegetables, and food-grade herbs marketed for weight loss, metabolism, blood sugar, cholesterol, and liver support) and AdaptogenX (a ginseng-based capsule marketed for stress-induced hair loss, greying, energy, and stress resistance). It is positioned for overall metabolic wellness, weight management, and hair/scalp health, priced at $167.00 CAD, and is currently backordered.
