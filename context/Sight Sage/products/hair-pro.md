## Summary

Hair Pro is a Canadian-made hair-health supplement from SightSage that combines traditional herbal extracts (Fleeceflower Root, ginseng, pumpkin seed, ginkgo biloba) with B vitamins (including biotin) and vitamin D3. It is marketed as a natural alternative to Rogaine intended to help prevent hair loss, support scalp health, fortify follicles, treat alopecia, and promote natural hair growth, taken as 3 capsules daily with food.

## Key Benefits

- Fortifies hair follicles and reduces thinning
- Helps prevent hair loss and promote natural hair growth
- Supports scalp health
- Nourishes hair follicles and improves blood circulation to the scalp (positioned as "Rogaine's natural alternative")
