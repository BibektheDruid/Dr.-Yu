## Summary

Adaptogen-X is a Canadian-made daily adaptogen capsule supplement (60 capsules/bottle) from SightSage, marketed to reduce cortisol levels and fight stress-induced hair loss and premature greying by addressing hormonal imbalances. It blends several ginsengs (Red, American, Siberian), Schisandra Chinensis, and Mai Dong, and is positioned for people dealing with conditions such as PCOS, thyroid issues, and perimenopause. It is priced at $78.00 CAD, rated 4.8/5 from 49 reviews, and is currently backordered.

- Reduces cortisol levels, helping manage stress-induced hair loss
- Reduces grey hairs and stops hair loss
- Strengthens roots and enhances hair vitality
- Treats hormonal imbalances behind hair loss and premature grey/white hairs
- Positioned as optimal for people dealing with PCOS, thyroid issues, and perimenopause
