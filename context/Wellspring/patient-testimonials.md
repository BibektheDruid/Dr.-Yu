# Wellspring Clinic — Patient Testimonials

> Verbatim testimonials and outcome stories extracted from **tcmrp.com/patient-stories/** (and the homepage), 2026-06-30. All conditions are Retinitis Pigmentosa (RP) unless noted.
> **Compliance note:** the clinic explicitly states results vary and the protocol is *not a cure* — see the disclaimer in [wellspring-brand-kit-context.md](wellspring-brand-kit-context.md). Use testimonials with that framing.

## Featured stories

**Adriana** — *"After 3 years my vision was so stable and so good that I was so confident. I didn't even remember I had RP."*
Dealing with severe night blindness and difficulty walking. Dr. Yu recognized RP and devised a protocol to stabilize her sight. Initially skeptical, she noticed significant night-vision improvement; after three years her vision stabilized, allowing her to drive and walk her dog independently. Stopped treatment in 2003; vision remains stable. *(Also the clinic's RP-origin patient.)*

**Geoff** — *"It's working and my eyes are not degenerating, so my vision is completely stable."*
Diagnosed with RP in 2009 and told by Western doctors he would eventually go blind. After starting the protocol, his visual field recovered enough to regain his driver's license and return to skiing.

**Sharyn** (Florida) — *"The big and important thing for me was that my visual field began to fill in."*
A milestone RP patient: in 1990 she became the first person in the world to undergo RP genetic testing, identifying the Rhodopsin P23H mutation. After 15 months of treatment, her visual field expanded from ~15° to 180°, with significant night-vision improvement and no longer needing contact lenses.

**Tracy** — *"I guess for me, the most important thing that he has done for me is he's allowed me to see my children."*
Told to train with a walking cane when declared legally blind at 32. A mother of three, after the protocol her vision stabilized with significant peripheral-vision improvement; she no longer trips, her headaches are gone, and she returned to teaching.

**Esther** — *"Every day was an amazing moment because every day I felt a miracle happening."*
Vision deteriorated as a teenager, costing her driver's license and threatening her teaching career. After the protocol her peripheral vision expanded dramatically and night vision improved significantly — she could see yellow again (important for teaching). Her husband calls it "miraculous."

**Sam** — *"Night glare, day glare has improved substantially. I don't even notice them anymore. I see better at nighttime."*
Tried supplements and light therapy with continued deterioration. After the intensive protocol, reported significant improvements in peripheral vision, night vision, visual acuity, and reduced light sensitivity day and night.

**Adrien** (Ireland) — *"I would be recommending this to anybody. It's definitely worth it."*
Came in with zero peripheral vision after being told by doctors in Hong Kong, China, Australia, America, and Europe that there was no cure. After the protocol his vision improved significantly; he looks forward to playing games with his children.
